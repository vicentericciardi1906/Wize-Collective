Our Philosophy

Holistic health
is a journey,
not a destination

Wize Collective was born from a deep belief that true wellbeing encompasses every layer of human experience. Founded by Xana Nunes, we weave together the wisdom of traditional coaching, breathwork, movement, and ceremony into experiences that nourish body, mind, and soul.

Our retreats and programs invite you into a slower pace — a space where transformation happens naturally, gently, and permanently. Portuguese in roots. Universal in reach.
